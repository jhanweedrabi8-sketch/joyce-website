My Journey

All About Joyce

Hello! My name is Joyce Andrea C. Sayson. I was born on September 10, 2007, and I grew up in the beautiful municipality of Matanao, Davao del Sur.

I am the youngest in our family of four, and I share my educational journey with my older sister who also attends university. Growing up in a close-knit family has shaped my values and my passion for connecting with others.

From Childhood Dreams to College Goals: My journey through education has been filled with curiosity and wonder. Now, as a first-year student, I'm actively working towards my dream of becoming a dedicated English teacher who can make a difference in students' lives.

Who I Am

About Me

📸

I'm a first-year college student pursuing Bachelor of Secondary Education, Major in English. My passion for literature and language drives my commitment to becoming an inspiring educator.

I believe that education is more than just textbooks and exams. It's about fostering critical thinking, nurturing creativity, and helping students discover their unique voice through language and literature.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating inclusive, engaging classrooms where every student feels valued and empowered to express themselves. I'm dedicated to making literature come alive and showing students why the English language matters.

Vision

Teaching Philosophy

"To teach English is not merely to instruct students on grammar rules and vocabulary, but to offer them a key to understanding human experiences, cultures, and diverse perspectives. Literature is a mirror that reflects our world and a window that shows us others."

As a future educator, my mission is to create an interactive environment where students don't just memorize texts, but learn to critique, appreciate, and actively engage with the stories that shape our society.
